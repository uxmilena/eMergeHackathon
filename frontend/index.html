California requires expense reimbursement for all remote work costs. Your current policy does not address this. Additionally, New York requires 72-hour advance notice for schedule changes. Oregon mandates predictive scheduling provisions for remote teams over 10 employees.

BEFORE

Employees working remotely are responsible for maintaining their own workspace equipment and internet connectivity. The company does not provide reimbursement for home office expenses unless pre-approved by management.

AFTER

Employees working remotely are responsible for maintaining their own workspace. The company will reimburse all reasonable and necessary remote work expenses as required by applicable state law, including but not limited to internet, phone, and equipment costs for employees in CA, NY, OR, and other applicable states.
